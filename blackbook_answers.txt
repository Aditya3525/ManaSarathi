29. What happens when user sends a message?
- Request is validated.
- Crisis language detection runs first.
- AI response is generated through chat service and provider routing/fallback logic.
- Conversation/message data is persisted.
- Smart replies/recommendations/metadata may be attached.
- Crisis path can return de-escalation/escalation response and record crisis events.

30. What happens when user logs mood?
- Authenticated request hits mood endpoint.
- Mood is normalized/validated (including legacy mapping from emotion groups/emotions).
- Entry is stored with fields like mood, emotion, intensity, trigger, notes.
- Stats endpoints aggregate distribution, streak, and trend-period metrics.

31. How are recommendations generated?
- Focus areas are derived from assessment insights, sentiment keywords/emotions, mood trend, and wellness context.
- Content/practices are filtered by approach and focus-area relevance.
- Items are ranked/curated with reasons.
- Fallback suggestions are returned when strong matches are not available.
